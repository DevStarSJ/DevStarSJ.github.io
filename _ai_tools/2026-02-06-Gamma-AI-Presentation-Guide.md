## Crafting Effective Prompts

### Basic Prompt
> Create a presentation about climate change

### Better Prompt
> Create a 10-slide presentation about climate change impacts on agriculture. Include statistics, solutions, and a call to action. Target audience: policy makers. Tone: professional but urgent.

### Prompt Elements

- **Topic**: What the presentation is about
- **Length**: Number of slides or sections
- **Audience**: Who will view it
- **Tone**: Professional, casual, educational
- **Specifics**: Data, examples, structure

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start with structure**: Outline key points before generating
2. **Be specific in prompts**: Details improve output quality
3. **Iterate, don't restart**: Refine existing content
4. **Use AI suggestions**: Let Gamma enhance your content
5. **Check flow**: Ensure narrative makes sense
6. **Less is more**: Don't overcrowd cards
7. **Preview often**: See how it looks in presentation mode
