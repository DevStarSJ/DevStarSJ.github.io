## What is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is an **AI-powered transcription and meeting assistant** that automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes your meetings. It joins your Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams calls as a participant and delivers searchable notes within minutes of the meeting ending.

### Why Teams Switch to Otter

- **Real-time Transcription**: Live captions during meetings
- **Auto-join Meetings**: Otter joins your calendar events
- **Speaker Identification**: Knows who said what
- **AI Summaries**: Key points and action items extracted
- **Search Everything**: Find any meeting by keyword

## Key Features

### 1. OtterPilot

Your AI meeting assistant that:

- Automatically joins scheduled meetings
- Records audio and video
- Transcribes in real-time
- Identifies different speakers
- Generates summary after meeting ends

### 2. Live Transcription

See words appear as people speak:

```
[10:03 AM] Sarah Chen: Let's discuss the Q2 marketing 
budget. I think we should increase social media spend 
by 20%.

[10:03 AM] Mike Johnson: That's ambitious. What's the 
expected ROI on that increase?

[10:04 AM] Sarah Chen: Based on Q1 data, we're looking 
at 3x return on paid social campaigns.
```

## Getting Started

## Pro Tips

### 1. Optimize Speaker Identification

- Use voice enrollment feature
- Have participants speak at start
- Correct misidentifications (it learns)

### 2. Search Like a Pro

Use Otter's search operators:

```
"product launch"     → Exact phrase
speaker:sarah        → Specific person
date:2024-03-01      → Specific date
```

## Accuracy & Privacy

- **90-95% accuracy** in ideal conditions

  - Background noise
