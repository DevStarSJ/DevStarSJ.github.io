## Pro Tips

### 1. Script Writing

```
❌ Bad: "Hello. Welcome. Today we will discuss sales."

✅ Good: "Hey there! Welcome to today's session. 
We're diving into something exciting—how to 
double your sales using proven techniques."
```

Natural language = natural delivery.

### 2. Avatar Selection

- Match avatar to audience
- Business content → professional avatar
- Gen Z marketing → younger, casual presenter
- Global audience → ethnically diverse avatars

### 3. Engagement Techniques

- Keep videos under 3 minutes
- Change camera angles/backgrounds mid-video
- Add visual elements (text, images)
- Include calls-to-action

## Creating Your Custom Avatar

### Requirements

1. **Recording Space**: Good lighting, plain background
2. **Camera**: Webcam is fine, 1080p preferred
3. **Audio**: Quiet environment, clear speech
4. **Time**: 2 minutes of varied expressions

### Recording Tips

- Look directly at camera
- Move naturally (slight head movements)
- Show range of expressions
- Speak clearly at normal pace
- Include smiles and neutral expressions

### Processing

- Upload takes 5-10 minutes
- Avatar ready within 24 hours
- Review and approve before using
